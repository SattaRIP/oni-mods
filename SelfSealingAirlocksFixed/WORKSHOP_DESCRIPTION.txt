=== TITLE ===
Self-sealing Airlocks (U59 Fix)

=== DESCRIPTION (paste into uploader) ===
Doors block gas and liquid while they are closing and closed -- no more puff of chlorine sneaking through a pneumatic door mid-swing. A plain door becomes a true airlock. Internal-type doors and doors pinned Open keep vanilla behavior.

This is chromiumboy's classic Self-Sealing Airlocks, brought back from the dead a second time: the earlier "Self-sealing Airlocks Revived" recompile crashes on the current build (U59-740622) with "MissingMethodException: SimMessages.SetCellProperties(int, byte)" the moment any door spawns. This version is rebuilt from the original source against the current game API (the Sim cell-property calls gained a callback parameter) and ported to Harmony 2 / UserMod2.
