# Patreon post draft — More Clothing

**Title:** More Clothing — five new garments for ONI, from winter coats to a checkpoint-free Soft Suit (new mod!)

---

New Oxygen Not Included mod: **More Clothing**. 🧥

Your dupes deserve a wardrobe. Five new garments, tailored at the machines you
already own:

**At the Clothing Refashionator:**

- 🧥 **Winter Coat** — a Warm Sweater rebuilt into a thick insulated coat with
  markedly better cold protection.
- 🟡 **Soft Suit** — the star of the mod: a lightweight, **checkpoint-free**
  alternative to the Atmo Suit. Cold, heat and radiation protection, sealed
  against airborne germs and wetness, a big air reserve dupes actually breathe
  from (no more held breath until it runs low), fewer bathroom trips — and a
  helmet that **assembles piece by piece over the dupe's head** when their
  surroundings turn dangerous, then retracts when they're safe. No air tank:
  it delays suffocation rather than preventing it. Mid-game "good enough"
  gear by design.
- ✨ **Snazzy Swimwear** (+25 decor) and **Snazzy Rubber Boots** (+15 decor) —
  gold-sequin upgrades of the Bionic Booster Pack garments.

**At the Textile Loom:**

- 👞 **Shoes** (+15 decor) — black-and-gold dress shoes, pure flourish.

Standalone — no other mods required. The Soft Suit and Snazzy recipes use
Bionic Booster Pack garments as ingredients; Winter Coat and Shoes are
DLC-free.
