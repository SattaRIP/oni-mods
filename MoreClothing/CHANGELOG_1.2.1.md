# More Clothing v1.2.1 — The Soft Suit catches its breath

A small update sharpening how the Soft Suit's air reserve recovers, plus a
description fix.

## Soft Suit: faster to get back on your feet

The Soft Suit trades an Atmo Suit's oxygen tank for a big breath reserve —
your dupe just lasts a lot longer before running low. But recovering that
whole reserve used to happen at the normal breathing rate, so after a long
dive a dupe would spend an age topping back up before the bar looked healthy
again.

Now the refill is **two-tier**:

- **Up to a normal dupe's lungful** — full speed. A dupe who just surfaced
  or stepped out of danger is mission-ready fast, same as any un-suited dupe
  catching their breath.
- **The bonus reserve above that** — backfills passively at a gentler pace
  while they're in breathable oxygen.

So the suit stops feeling sluggish to "recharge": dupes bounce back to
functional immediately, and the deep-dive reserve quietly tops itself off in
the background.

## Fixes

- **Snazzy Swimwear description** corrected — it no longer claims to keep you
  "dry-footed." Swimwear has never granted feet protection; its real job is
  shrugging off the soggy-morale hit from wading through liquid. (For keeping
  bionic dupes un-shocked in water, that's Rubber Boots for puddles or the
  Soft Suit for full submersion — the Soft Suit's airtight seal makes its
  wearer immune at any depth.)
