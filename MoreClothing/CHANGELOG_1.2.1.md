# More Clothing v1.2.1 — Suit-friendly footwear & a smarter Soft Suit

A bug-fix and polish pass on top of 1.2.0.

## Fixes

- **Worn footwear no longer hijacks Atmo Suits.** In 1.2.0 the new visible
  Snazzy Rubber Boots / Shoes drew their foot art at a layer that could
  outrank a full suit, so a dupe wearing both an Atmo Suit *and* Snazzy boots
  ended up with the boots painted over the suit's feet (and, combined with
  the swimwear layer, the suit could look wrong or vanish, with stray marks
  near the head). All of More Clothing's worn art is now pinned to the normal
  clothing layer, so **any suit correctly renders on top** — boots and
  swimwear show when worn alone, and cleanly hide under an Atmo/Lead/Jet Suit.

## Soft Suit: faster to get back on your feet

The Soft Suit trades an Atmo Suit's oxygen tank for a big breath reserve, but
recovering that whole reserve used to happen at the normal breathing rate —
so after a long dive a dupe spent an age topping back up. Now the refill is
**two-tier**:

- **Up to a normal dupe's lungful** — full speed, so a dupe who just surfaced
  or left danger is mission-ready fast.
- **The bonus reserve above that** — backfills passively at a gentler pace
  while they're in breathable oxygen.

---

*More Clothing is standalone. The Soft Suit and Snazzy upgrades use Bionic
Booster Pack garments as ingredients; the Winter Coat, Shoes, and Mannequin
are DLC-free.*
