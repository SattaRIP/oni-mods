# More Clothing v1.2.1 — Suit compatibility & a smarter Soft Suit

A bug-fix and polish pass on top of 1.2.0, mostly around how the mod's gear
behaves alongside vanilla Atmo / Lead / Jet Suits, plus a friendlier Soft Suit.

## Fixes

- **Snazzy Swimwear no longer makes a suit invisible.** Worn under an Atmo
  Suit, the swimwear used to render over the top and hide the suit. It's been
  rebuilt on the standard vest base (like the Winter Coat), so it now tucks
  cleanly under any suit while keeping its function — feet-and-waist wet
  protection and the Sopping Wet / Soggy Feet immunities.

- **Bionic dupes stay safe in Soft Suits.** The Soft Suit's airtight seal is
  now held continuously while worn, so it can't be dropped by an equipment
  refresh or a save-load — bionic duplicants in Soft Suits no longer get
  shocked in water.

- **Soft Suit now blocks Popped Eardrums** (over-pressure) and **Intensely
  Bright** (sunlight burning in space), matching the vanilla suits.

- **Soft Suit + a real suit render correctly.** With an Atmo/Lead/Jet Suit worn
  over a Soft Suit, the suit's own helmet now shows and the Soft Suit's mask
  steps aside — previously the retract hid the suit's helmet too, leaving no
  helmet at all.

- **Snazzy Rubber Boots & Shoes no longer clash with suits.** The 1.2.0
  drawn-on boot art conflicted with worn suits, so for now the Snazzy footwear
  behaves like standard footwear (still craftable, still gives decor and
  protection) with no drawn-on art. The visible-boots look will return in a
  later update once the suit interaction is sorted.

## Soft Suit: faster to get back on your feet

The Soft Suit trades an Atmo Suit's oxygen tank for a big breath reserve, but
recovering that whole reserve used to happen at the normal breathing rate — so
after a long dive a dupe spent an age topping back up. The refill is now
**two-tier**: it catches up to a normal dupe's lungful at full speed (so
they're mission-ready fast), then quietly backfills the bonus reserve at a
gentler pace while in breathable oxygen.

---

*More Clothing is standalone. The Soft Suit and Snazzy upgrades use Bionic
Booster Pack garments as ingredients; the Winter Coat, Shoes, and Mannequin are
DLC-free.*
