# Patreon post draft — Longer Bridges

**Title:** Longer Bridges — gap-spanning bridges for every utility in ONI (new mod!)

---

I just released a new Oxygen Not Included mod: **Longer Bridges**. 🌉

Vanilla bridges only hop a single tile. Longer Bridges adds **2-tile and 3-tile
gap** variants for **every** utility type, so you can cross over wider runs
without awkward stacking:

- ✅ **Liquid Pipes** and **Gas Pipes**
- ✅ **Power Wires**, **Conductive Wires**, and **Insulated Conductive Wires**
- ✅ **Automation Wires** and **Automation Ribbons**
- ✅ **Conveyor Rails**
- ✅ If you run **Ronivans Legacy – Industrial Revolution**, its Logistic Solid,
  Heavy-Duty, and Heavy-Duty Joint Plate bridges get gap variants too —
  automatically, only when you have it

Every bridge sits in its **normal build-menu category**, in **English**, right
next to its one-tile counterpart.

**Fully standalone — no other mods required.** If you were using the older Magpie
Bridge (鹊桥) setup, the switch is **seamless**: the liquid/gas/wire bridges you
already placed keep working, no rebuild needed.
