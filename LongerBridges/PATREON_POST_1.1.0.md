# Patreon post draft — Longer Bridges 1.1.0

**Title:** Longer Bridges 1.1.0 — Wide Joint Plates: heavy wire through thick walls

**Attach:** `screenshots_jointplates.png` (the 6-plate showcase)

---

**Longer Bridges** just got its first big content update. 🔌🧱

Vanilla's Heavi-Watt Joint Plate only crosses ONE tile of wall — thick
insulated walls mean stacking plates and checkerboarding tiles. Not anymore:

- ⚡ **Heavi-Watt Joint Plate (2 Tiles / 3 Tiles)** — one building, one clean
  run of Heavi-Watt Wire through two or three tiles of wall
- ⚡ **Conductive Joint Plate (2 Tiles / 3 Tiles)** — same, for Conductive Wire
- ❄️ **Insulated versions (3 Tiles)** of both — these appear automatically if
  you also run the **Insulated Joint Plate [FIXED]** mod (which covers the 1-
  and 2-wide insulated plates; mine slot in right beside its "Long" entries).
  Bonus fix that mod lacks: deconstructing my plates properly restores the
  cells' normal heat transfer instead of leaving ghost insulation behind.

Details that matter:

- Same **research** as the vanilla plates — nothing unlocks early
- **Rotatable** — run heavy wire vertically through floors and ceilings too
- Art is composed from the vanilla plate per cell (no stretched, blurry
  rivets), so it looks native in-game
- Still **fully standalone** — the insulated variants are optional garnish,
  everything else works with zero dependencies
