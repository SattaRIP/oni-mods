# Patreon post draft — Magpie Bridges+

**Title:** Magpie Bridges+ — English names + every bridge in its right menu (new ONI mod!)

---

I just released a new Oxygen Not Included mod: **Magpie Bridges+**. 🐦‍⬛🌉

If you've used the excellent **Magpie Bridge (鹊桥)** mod, you know the pain:
the bridges have Chinese names and all pile into one unlabelled "Label" group in
the build menu. Magpie Bridges+ fixes that **and** adds a pile of new bridge
variants:

- ✅ Renames the base liquid / gas / wire / conductive-wire bridges to **English**
- ✅ Sorts **every** bridge into its correct build-menu category (Plumbing → Pipes,
  Ventilation → Pipes, Power → Wires) — no more "Label"
- ✅ Adds **2-tile and 3-tile gap** variants for **Automation Wires**,
  **Automation Ribbons**, and **Conveyor Rails**
- ✅ If you run **Ronivans Legacy – Industrial Revolution**, adds gap variants for
  its Logistic Solid, Heavy-Duty, and Heavy-Duty Joint Plate bridges too —
  automatically, only when you have it

**Requires** the original Magpie Bridge mod (it's listed as a Required Item, so
Steam grabs it for you). Ronivans Legacy is optional.
