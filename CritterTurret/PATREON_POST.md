# Patreon post draft — Critter Turret

**Title:** Critter Turret — automatic critter population control for ONI (new mod!)

---

New Oxygen Not Included mod: **Critter Turret**. 🔴🔫

Ranching produces more critters than you need, and culling the surplus by hand
(or with drowning chambers) is a chore. The Critter Turret is a **red robo-miner
that shoots critters instead of rock** — automatic, hands-off population
control:

- ✅ **Set a population threshold** on its side screen — it only opens fire while
  the critter count in its room is above your target, then stands down
- ✅ **Choose what it targets**: Adults, Adults & Babies, or Babies
- ✅ **Mounts on floor, wall, or ceiling** with a directional firing arc, just
  like the Robo-Miner — line of sight required
- ✅ Fires the **duplicant multitool's attack laser** — real beam particles and
  sound
- ✅ Kills drop meat and resources normally — it's a self-operating meat farm
- ✅ Has a **logic port** so you can disable it from automation
- ✅ Never targets Duplicants

Find it in the **Shipping** menu next to the Robo-Miner. 120 W, refined metal.

**Fully standalone — no other mods required.**
